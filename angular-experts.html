Why Angular

By watching the above list, you can easily make out that companies ranging from Fortune 100 to startups are using AngularJS for developing their websites, apps and products.

  • Instead of buidling separate Android, iOS, blackberry, windows app, just build a responsive Angular app and use it everywhere.
  • Ease of maintenance - When there is a problem, we don't want to spend hours debugging. we should be able to make day-to-day minor changes with ease.
  • Efficiency of building new features - When I want to build something new, does the framework help me or hinder me?
